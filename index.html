Lebogang Saite

Singer Lebogang Goitsimang Saite's healing music infuses smooth R&B vocals and sweet gospel harmonies into contemporary acapella rhythms - creating a unique brand of conscious music and soulful psalms that uplifts and inspires. Lebogang Aims at Peace Process, in a continuation of his work to bring peace, enlightenment and healing to the world through music in these troublous times.
